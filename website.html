Who benefits

Designed for people
who need it most

Not a generic calorie tracker. A clinical-grade nutrition coach built around the health realities of Indian patients.

🫘
Chronic kidney disease patients
CKD patients must carefully restrict protein, potassium, phosphorus and sodium. One wrong meal can cause dangerous hyperkalemia. NutriCoach monitors every nutrient against KDIGO 2024 clinical limits in real time.
Protein ≤ 0.7g/kg Potassium ≤ 2000mg Phosphorus ≤ 800mg Sodium ≤ 1500mg
🩸
Type 2 diabetes patients
Consistent carbohydrate management is critical for blood glucose control. The system tracks carb intake against ADA 2024 standards and suggests low-GI Indian alternatives after every meal.
Carbs 130–225g/day Fiber ≥ 30g/day Low GI foods preferred
❤️
Anyone aged 30–60 years
Harvard research shows people in the top 20% of diet quality have an 86% greater chance of healthy aging at 70. Start building those habits today, before chronic disease begins.
Prevention-focused Longevity coaching Weight management

Technology

Research-grade AI stack

Five specialised AI components fused into one seamless pipeline — each chosen for a specific, justified reason.

🎯
YOLOv8
Multi-food object detection with bounding boxes. Detects all items in one photo. Deployed via ONNX for CPU inference without TensorFlow.
Computer Vision
🧠
RAG + ChromaDB
Retrieval-Augmented Generation using Sentence-Transformers embeddings. Retrieves KDIGO & ADA guidelines to ground every LLM response.
NLP · Knowledge Retrieval
🤖
Llama 3.3 (Groq)
70B parameter LLM via Groq API. Fast inference, high quality coaching responses grounded by RAG-retrieved clinical context.
Large Language Model
📊
Random Forest
Machine learning risk predictor trained on longitudinal dietary features. Generates 0-100 risk scores for 4 chronic diseases.
Machine Learning
🗄️
SQLite
Lightweight embedded database storing every meal as a permanent health record. Enables weekly pattern analysis and longitudinal risk prediction.
Data Storage
⚕️
Clinical Rule Engine
Evidence-based condition-specific nutrient limits from KDIGO 2024 and ADA 2024. Real-time alert generation with per-nutrient progress tracking.
Clinical AI
📈
EfficientNetB3
Fallback classification model fine-tuned on Indian food images via transfer learning. 68.6% accuracy with 89.75% top-5 accuracy.
Deep Learning
🌐
Streamlit
Production-ready web dashboard with 4 tabs — meal logging, weekly analytics, health risk, and AI coaching. Zero-cost deployment.
Web Application
